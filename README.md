<h2>🔐 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness</h2>
<h3>This task focuses on understanding cybercrime, especially UPI/Online Payment Fraud, and learning how to stay safe while using online payment systems. UPI fraud is a common cybercrime where scammers trick people into sending money or sharing sensitive information such as OTPs or UPI PINs through fake messages, calls, or QR codes. Many users, especially students and online shoppers, become victims due to a lack of awareness.

In this task, a case study was created to explain how such fraud happens in real-life situations. It shows how a simple mistake, like scanning a QR code to receive money, can lead to financial loss. A prevention checklist was also prepared to highlight important safety measures such as not sharing personal details, avoiding unknown links, verifying payment details, and using strong passwords with two-factor authentication.

Overall, this task helped in understanding how cybercrimes occur and how they can be prevented by following basic safety practices. It improved awareness about responsible digital behavior and the importance of staying alert while using online platforms.

In case of any cyber fraud, it is important to report immediately through official channels.
